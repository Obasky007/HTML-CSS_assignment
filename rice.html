Naija Rice

White Rice with Stew

Recipe:

  1. Wash rice thoroughly.
  2. Boil rice with salt until soft.
  3. Drain and keep aside.
  4. Blend tomatoes, peppers, onions.
  5. Heat oil in pot.
  6. Fry tomato mix until thick.
  7. Add seasoning cubes, curry, thyme.
  8. Add cooked beef or chicken.
  9. Pour in stock and simmer.
  10. Serve stew over white rice.

Serving Suggestion: Enjoy with fried plantain, boiled eggs, or a chilled soft drink.

Jollof Rice

Recipe:

  1. Wash and parboil rice, set aside.
  2. Blend tomatoes, red peppers, onions.
  3. Boil blended mix until thick.
  4. Heat oil, add sliced onions.
  5. Fry tomato mix in the oil.
  6. Add seasoning, curry, thyme, bay leaf.
  7. Pour in chicken stock or water.
  8. Stir in rice gently.
  9. Cover pot, cook on low heat.
  10. Fluff rice, serve with meat or plantain.

Serving Suggestion: Best served with grilled chicken, salad, and cold drinks at parties.

Fried Rice

Recipe:

  1. Wash and parboil rice.
  2. Boil rice in stock until almost done.
  3. Cut vegetables: carrot, peas, green beans.
  4. Dice chicken liver or beef.
  5. Heat oil in pan.
  6. Stir-fry vegetables and liver.
  7. Add curry and seasoning.
  8. Mix in cooked rice.
  9. Stir well until rice is colorful.
  10. Serve hot with chicken or fish.

Serving Suggestion: Pair with grilled prawns, roasted chicken, or moi moi.

Coconut Rice

Recipe:

  1. Wash and parboil rice, set aside.
  2. Break coconut, extract milk (or use canned).
  3. Blend onions, peppers (optional).
  4. Heat oil in a pot, add sliced onions.
  5. Fry blended mix until reduced.
  6. Add coconut milk and some stock.
  7. Season with curry, thyme, salt, cubes.
  8. Pour in the rice, stir gently.
  9. Cover and cook on low heat.
  10. Serve with fried fish, chicken, or plantain.

Serving Suggestion: Goes perfectly with fried fish, coleslaw, or spicy pepper sauce.
